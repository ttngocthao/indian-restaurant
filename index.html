About Us

We have a mission!

We are an independently owned bar and restaurant and free from any brewery ties or controls.

Our bar and restaurant is based on the belief that our customers needs are of the utmost importance. Our entire team is committed to meeting those needs. Every effort has been made to provide a comfortable atmosphere for families, groups of friends, couples or just someone popping in for a drink.

The premises has been inspected by the Food Standards Agency and has achieved a food hygiene rating of 4, just one away from perfect. We are of course continually upgrading equipment and working towards achieving the perfect score of 5.
